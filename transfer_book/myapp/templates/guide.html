Guidelines for account creation/authentication

Signing up

  • The username must be unique among all users. By default, it allows alphanumeric characters and the following special characters: @, ., +, -, and _.
  • Password must be minimum 8 characters long.
  • Password must not be very common or similar to your other information.
  • Password must have at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, and one digit.
    •

    Logging In

  • Make sure you have an account before logging in.
  • Enter correct username and password, both fields are case sensitive.
    •

    Creating/Updating or Deleting Reecords:

  • You need to be logged in to store your records.
  • The records that you delete are deleted permanently and with no way to restore them.
